Scramled Eggs Recipe

Fluffy and tender scambled eggs

    Ingredients
  • Two eggs
  • salt and pepper
  • sour cream
  • butter
    Directions
  1. Place a slice of butter into the pan at medium heat
  2. crack two eggs and place it into the pan
  3. stir gently while adding a spoon of sour cream
  4. Enjoy!
