Ramen Recipe

A delicous bowl of rmaen you can make at home!

    Ingredients
  • Instant Noodles
  • Egg
  • Scallions
  • ground ginger
    Directions
  1. Bring two cups of water in a pot to boiling
  2. Placed the instant ramen in the pot for 2 minutes
  3. Add the included Ramen seasoning, Scallions and ground ginger into the pot. Then stir to mix the seasoning together.
  4. Let the ramen sit for another minute in the pot and then finally add the egg and stir the noddles and egg for 14 seconds.
  5. Enjoy!
